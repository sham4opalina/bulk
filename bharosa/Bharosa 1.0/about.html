Our mission

Deliver honest, effective & commission free financial products that change lives

At Bharosa Club, we genuinely believe its time for some disruptive thinking in the financial industry.

Become a Member & Stay Updated

Commission based selling, inadequate technological innovation, cluttered product offerings & complicated processes have contributed to general distrust and abysmal adoption of genuinely life changing financial products.

Our dream is to change the status quo by creating a shared utility which addresses the last mile problem for simple and honest products. With world class technology and a large army of on-ground assistants we hope to serve millions. In other words a winning formula for customers & businesses.

Founding principles

Our Constitution

Our founding principles are the essence of our internal value system and continue to guide us

Be Universal

Bharosa will serve members from all economic strata. In households, from the poorest to the richest, in businesses from smallest to the largest.

Be Member Driven

Bharosa club will serve the interests of its members, both households and businesses. It will not take commissions or advertising and will treat all of its members as friends and family valuing their well being above all. Creating member delight and value are paramount at Bharosa Club.

Be Respectful

Bharosa will not abuse member data and will not release individual member data unless the member expressly authorises it. Bharosa Club will respect all the rules , regulations and laws of the jurisdiction(s) it operates in.

Be Constructive

Bharosa club will provide its members with a platform, which will use the power of numbers, to enable positive change in a constructive manner.

Be Profitable

Bharosa club will help business members cut costs, by creating a shared last mile utility, which will enable businesses to serve more households.

Be World Class

Bharosa is a for profit institute. It wants to make billions and help millions as only then will it be able to attract the best and brightest to work for Bharosa.

Our team

Makes us who we are

We are a group of like minded people working towards empowering households

Sanjay Bhargava

Chairman & CEO

Sanjay has always believed that a company can “Make Billions and Help Millions”. He was on the founding team of PayPal and quoting Elon Musk “Sanjay Bhargava figured out the world’s first low cost method of authenticating terrestrial bank accounts, which was critical to making the PayPal Business model work". In an interview with WSJ he stated that "The biggest obstacle in rural India is to change the culture from one of dependence to one of true independence", he went on to start the Universal Financial Access (UFA) movement in India and IIC Fellowship with the University of Chicago. Sanjay is an alumnus of IIT B, IIM A, Stanford University and the Citibank Group.

He believes that democratization of technology will help Bharosa Club make a big contribution to empowering households across India.

Prakash Pai

President, Operations & Technology

Prakash was the President & Global Head, Products at Nucleus Software for the last 16 years and was responsible for building Finnone, India’s leading retail lending financial product.

He headed the Technology Division for Nokia Money in India, which was one of India’s first mobile wallets, with over 1 million customers.

An alumnus of IIT Delhi, he is passionate about empowering households and helping them be financially independent.

Anita Bhargava

Executive VP, Members

Anita Bhargava headed the Data Warehousing group of PayPal till 2004. After returning to India, she started a movement called 'Let's Do It! Delhi' where she got over 50,000 residents of Delhi to participate in 125 cleanups across Delhi.

She has been active in working with politicians and bureaucracy to help them with talent and ideas to achieve "Big Hairy Audacious Goals" (iicworld.org).

Anita is a believer in the power of the people - technology today gives us tools to help realise the dreams of many, but all this needs the compassion and involvement of people.

Shantanu Kumar

Executive VP, Marketing

Shantanu brings with him over 16 years of experience as a strategic planner, having led the planning function across leading advertising and public relations agencies in India.

Most recently, he was part of the founding team of I:Connect, followed by stint at Concept PR as a Senior Vice President. During his career, he has worked on several blue-chip brands across industries and geographies.

An Economics Honors graduate from St. Stephens College and an alumnus of MICA, he strongly believes that lack of motivation, is the biggest stumbling block when it comes to achieving financial empowerment. That’s what he aims to change.

Ashish Sardesai

Executive VP, Shared Utility

Ashish brings on board 20 years of experience across functions spanning, Strategic Alliances with Banks, BPO’s, NBFC’s and Heading profit operations. An accomplished Sales & Marketing professional with rich experience of establishing large distribution networks and channel sales management across Life Insurance companies, FMCG and IT education sector.

During his tenure with TATA AIA Life Insurance & then Edelweiss Tokio Life as Country Head Partnership Channel, he has been successful in building robust value propositions for all stakeholders.

Ashish’s obsession with ‘protecting the customer’s interests’ makes him an invaluable member of the founding team at Bharosa Club.

Puneet Chandra

CFO

Puneet is a Chartered Accountant by qualification and was a successful entrepreneur for over a decade before embarking on a 15 year stint with Wipro.

As the Global Head for Corporate Business Services at Wipro BPO, he was instrumental in setting up delivery centers in Brazil, Mexico, US, Poland, Romania and China. Later as the CMO for Wipro, he spearheaded the transformation of the Corporate brand across stakeholders & geographies.
