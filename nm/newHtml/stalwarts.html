Stalwarts Say

Chandrajit Banerjee,
Director General, CII
July 06, 2015
Prime Minister Modi's visit to Central Asia is a landmark event to revitalize our ancient links with the region. CII estimates that trade with the five Central Asian countries of Kazakhstan, Turkmenistan, Tajikistan, Uzbekistan and Kyrgyzstan can multiply manifold from the small base of $1.4 billion currently.
